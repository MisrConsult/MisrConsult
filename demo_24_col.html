history MC

"MISR CONSULT for Environmental and Infrastructure Studies", is a well known consultant office established by Dr. Sameh Abdel-Gawad since 1986, and working in the fields of Environmental Engineering and Infrastructures in Egypt. Dr. Abdel-Gawad runs the consultant office in addition to his position as a Professor in the Faculty of Eng. at Cairo University.

Prof. Dr. Abdel-Gawad has more than 31 years of experience in water pollution control studies and project management. He has worked several projects; both in Canada and Egypt, in this field, and has also published more than 31 scientific papers in both National and International Journals and conferences. He teaches Hydraulics and Environmental Engineering to both graduate and undergraduate levels.
